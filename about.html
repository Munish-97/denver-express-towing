About Denver Express Towing

Premium towing and emergency recovery services trusted by the Denver metropolitan area.

Who We Are

Denver's Premier Towing & Recovery Fleet

Founded on the principles of speed, safety, and unwavering professionalism, Denver Express Towing has grown from a single truck operation into a comprehensive emergency recovery fleet.

We believe that every driver deserves a premium experience, even during a breakdown. That's why we heavily invest in the latest equipment, extensive operator training, and modern dispatch technology to ensure your vehicle is handled with the utmost care.

Our Mission

At Denver Express Towing, our mission is to redefine the towing industry standard. We provide fast, damage-free, and highly professional towing and roadside assistance across Denver. We understand that needing a tow is often stressful, which is why we approach every dispatch with urgency, transparency, and a commitment to customer care.

Whether you're stranded on I-25 in the middle of the night or need a luxury vehicle transported securely, our state-of-the-art fleet and highly trained operators are ready 24/7.

Why Denver Drivers Trust Us
